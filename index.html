Guatemala Agua Project

Join Salem Montessori School as we raise money to help two families living in Guatemala gain access to water.

About the Agua Project

The Guatemala Agua Project was created to bring a well to two families in rural Guatemala. Currently in Guatemala, the water situation is less than ideal. In fact, across the world, a child dies every 15 seconds due to water borne illnesses. The cost to install a personal well for a single family is about $1,300. Therefore, the cost of a well for two families is about $2,600.
